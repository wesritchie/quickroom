Methodology Notes

How this page is built and the sharp distinctions to keep in mind when reading it.
Apples to apples
Every number on this page is retail Dutchie data. Net Sales = sum of three stores' Dutchie net sales for the quarter. Gross Sales = pre-discount. Cost is product COGS from the Dutchie closing-report — it does not include occupancy, payroll, or other store opex. So Retail Product Margin here (~53–57% portfolio) is the SKU-level margin, useful for store-vs-store unit economics. The Profitability page is where you go for full P&L gross margin, NOI, and §471(c)-adjusted economic margin (~59.7%).
Reconciliation flag
Groton FY 2025 net sales pulled from Dutchie API: $544,015. The value previously stored in operations-data.js: $426,192. That's a $117,823 discrepancy. Dutchie API is authoritative — operations-data.js Groton number is stale and should be updated. Dracut and Pepperell reconcile within $1 across both sources.
