Eight quarters of consolidated revenue, COGS, gross profit, and NOI. Includes both retail (Dutchie) and farm (wholesale, exchange) revenue rolled up through QuickBooks.
Scope note
FY 2024 reflects NECC standalone P&L (pre-MACC consolidation). FY 2025 onward reflects fully-consolidated NECC + MACC. Year-over-year comparisons across this boundary include scope expansion, not just same-store change. Same-quarter comparisons within FY 2025 (e.g., Q4 vs Q3) are comparable on a like-for-like basis.
